Luxabot Bot Interworkings

In the realm of automated cryptocurrency trading, embarking on a journey with Luxabot is akin to having a sophisticated companion by your side, navigating the tumultuous waters of the crypto market. The meticulous process of creating and configuring your bot is the inaugural step towards a potentially profitable and insightful trading experience. Upon completing this initial setup, Luxabot transitions you into a phase of enhanced visibility and control over your trading bot.

As you finalize the creation process, Luxabot seamlessly integrates the bot onto your account homepage. Herein lies a gateway, an 'info' button, that takes you to a comprehensive bot information page. This page is meticulously designed to serve as a control center for reviewing, modifying, and understanding your bot's settings and performance.

A salient feature on this page is the unique bot ID, a crucial identifier that distinguishes your bot amidst potentially multiple bots you may operate over time. This individualized bot ID is pivotal for precise tracking, management, and identification, ensuring a streamlined user experience.

Transitioning into the heart of the bot information page, you are greeted with a robust performance metrics table. This table is a repository of critical data reflecting your bot's trading performance. It encapsulates metrics such as the number of trades closed, total trade profit, average time spent in trades, and average trade profit. Further, it displays your starting capital alongside the current total capital, providing a clear financial trajectory since the inception of your trading bot's journey.

Augmenting the numerical data is an interactive graph, providing a visual narrative of your bot’s performance trajectory over time. This graphical representation is invaluable for dissecting trends, understanding the bot's behavior, and deriving insightful interpretations that could inform any necessary adjustments to your trading strategy.

Besides the performance metrics, the bot information page houses a dedicated console that meticulously logs and displays all completed orders. Each entry in this console bears a timestamp, detailing the execution date, quantity, time, and price of the orders. This console is a live chronicle of your bot's actions in the market, ensuring you are always in the loop regarding each trade executed, and the conditions under which they were carried out.

This bot information page is a manifestation of Luxabot's commitment to delivering a user-centric, transparent, and empowering trading platform. It consolidates all pivotal information and control mechanisms at your fingertips, fostering an environment conducive for informed decision-making and precise control over your automated trading journey.

The amalgamation of a user-friendly interface, detailed performance metrics, and real-time order logging on the bot information page epitomizes Luxabot’s objective of demystifying automated cryptocurrency trading. It ensures users are equipped with the necessary tools and insights to navigate the crypto market confidently, with a clear understanding of their bot’s performance and the potential to fine-tune their strategies to align with their financial objectives.

In essence, the bot information page is more than just a dashboard; it's a command center, embodying Luxabot's ethos of combining sophisticated automated trading technology with an intuitive and informative user interface. This detailed post-creation dashboard amplifies the bond of trust and control between you and Luxabot, ensuring a transparent, informative, and rewarding automated trading experience. Through this holistic approach, Luxabot reaffirms its dedication to making the realm of automated cryptocurrency trading an enriching and rewarding endeavor for its user community.
